Overview

Muslim Flow is a comprehensive spiritual companion app. It provides Quranic text, prayer times based on location, Qibla direction, and educational quizzes. Most features operate offline using a local database, while certain features like prayer times and Qibla require specific device permissions to function accurately.

Core Privacy Principles:
  • Offline First: Your Quran reading progress and personal favorites are stored locally on your device.
  • Location Privacy: Location data is used only locally to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction. We do not store or track your movements.
  • No Personal Accounts: We do not require you to create an account or provide an email address to use the app.
  • Data Security: We use industry-standard services for crash reporting and app performance monitoring.

Information Collection and Use

Location Information

To provide accurate prayer times and Qibla direction, the app requires access to your device's location. This information is processed **locally** on your device using the Adhan library. We do not transmit your precise location to our servers.

Local Data (Stored on Your Device)

  • Quran reading progress (Last Read)
  • Bookmarked Ayah (Favorites)
  • Prayer tracking history
  • App preferences (Theme, Language, Notification settings)
  • Quiz progress and scores

Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: Required for app updates and core functionality.
  • Firebase Crashlytics & Analytics: Used to monitor app stability and understand feature usage patterns in an anonymized way.
  • Google Play Billing: Used to securely process PRO subscriptions.

Log Data

We want to inform you that whenever you use our Service, in a case of an error in the app we collect data and information (through third party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

Permissions

  • Storage/Media: Used if you choose to download Quran audio for offline listening.
